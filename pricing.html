FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What cloud platforms do you support?

We currently support AWS and GCP, with Azure support coming in 2027 H1. Our AI engine connects via secure API to scan your cloud infrastructure without requiring any agent installation.

What compliance frameworks are covered?

CyberDiagram generates reports mapped to PDPA (Singapore/Thailand/Malaysia), ISO 27001, PCI-DSS, and CIS Benchmarks. Reports are audit-ready and formatted for both technical teams and non-technical stakeholders.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. All subscriptions are billed monthly with no long-term commitment. You can cancel at any time and your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. See our refund policy for details.

How is this different from Wiz or other CSPM tools?

CyberDiagram is purpose-built for SMBs. We combine AI-driven penetration testing with visual attack path diagrams that non-technical CEOs can understand, at a price point accessible to companies with 20-50 employees. We also offer an open-source option for capable teams.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Our Basic plan is free forever and lets you evaluate our scanning capabilities. For Enterprise and Flagship plans, contact us to arrange a pilot engagement tailored to your environment.
