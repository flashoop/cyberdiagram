# CyberDiagram: AI-Powered Cloud Penetration Testing for SMEs

## Executive Summary

CyberDiagram.com delivers intelligent, automated penetration testing specifically designed for cloud-based SME operations. Our platform discovers vulnerabilities and intrusion vectors across cloud infrastructure, presenting findings through intuitive visual diagrams that enable rapid remediation and informed security decisions.

---

## The Problem We Solve

Small and medium-sized enterprises face a critical security gap:
- **Limited Security Resources**: Most SMEs lack dedicated security teams
- **Complex Cloud Environments**: Multi-cloud deployments create blind spots
- **Compliance Pressures**: Growing regulatory requirements (GDPR, R155, industry standards)
- **Costly Breaches**: Average data breach costs continue to rise, disproportionately impacting smaller businesses
- **Traditional Pentest Limitations**: Manual pentests are expensive, infrequent, and quickly outdated

---
